- #ENGL
-
- Requests
	- Can / Could + S + V
	- e.g.) Can you pass me that book, please?
-
- Suggestions
	- S + can / could + V
	- e.g.) We could do a writing activity in today's class.
-
- Offer
	- Can / Could + S + V
	- e.g.) Can I explain the activity to you?
-
- Ability
	- S + can / could + V
	- e.g.) Alex can speak Spanish.
